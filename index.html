CRISTIANO RONALDO UNLEASHES INCREDIBLE BICYCLE KICK TO BAG HIS 910th CAREER GOAL

While Bruno Fernandes smashed in the third, Ronaldo set up a decisive pass for Pedro Neto to score within the inside post.
