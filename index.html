CRISTIANO RONALDO UNLEASHES INCREDIBLE BICYCLE KICK TO BAG HIS 910th CAREER GOAL
Cristiano Ronaldo 910th Career Goal, Portugal vs Poland: Cristiano Ronaldo rolled back years as he unleashed a memorable moment during Portugal's Nations League match against Poland on Friday. Ronaldo scored an incredible goal through a jaw-dropping overhead kick to help Portugal beat Poland 5-0 at the Estadio Do Dragao in Porto. After a goalless first half, Rafael Leao broke the deadlock just before the hour with a header. Ronaldo then doubled the lead from the penalty spot in the 72nd minute. While Bruno Fernandes smashed in the third, Ronaldo set up a decisive pass for Pedro Neto to score within the inside post. The memorable instance came when Ronaldo made the most of a dipping cross from Vitinha before beating goalkeeper Marcin Bulka with a close-range overhead kick.
